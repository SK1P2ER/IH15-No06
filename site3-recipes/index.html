Fluffy Banana Pancakes

Light and fluffy pancakes made with ripe bananas and oat flour

⏱️ 15 min 2 servings Easy

Fluffy Banana Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup oat flour
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Mix mashed bananas with milk and vanilla
  2. Add flour, baking powder, and cinnamon
  3. Heat pan and pour batter
  4. Cook until bubbles form, then flip
  5. Serve with maple syrup and berries
Lunch

Rainbow Buddha Bowl

Colorful bowl packed with quinoa, roasted veggies, and tahini dressing

⏱️ 30 min 2 servings Medium

Rainbow Buddha Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa, cooked
  • 1 sweet potato, cubed
  • 1 cup chickpeas
  • 2 cups kale, massaged
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 3 tbsp tahini dressing

Instructions:

  1. Roast sweet potato and chickpeas at 400°F for 25 min
  2. Cook quinoa according to package
  3. Massage kale with lemon juice
  4. Assemble bowl with all ingredients
  5. Drizzle with tahini dressing
Dinner

Creamy Mushroom Pasta

Rich and creamy pasta with sautéed mushrooms and cashew sauce

⏱️ 25 min 4 servings Easy

Creamy Mushroom Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz pasta of choice
  • 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup cashews, soaked
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions
  2. Blend cashews with broth until creamy
  3. Sauté mushrooms and garlic until golden
  4. Add cashew cream and nutritional yeast
  5. Toss with pasta and serve hot
Dessert

Chocolate Avocado Mousse

Decadent chocolate mousse made silky smooth with avocado

⏱️ 10 min 4 servings Easy

Chocolate Avocado Mousse

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of sea salt

Instructions:

  1. Blend all ingredients until smooth
  2. Taste and adjust sweetness if needed
  3. Chill in refrigerator for 30 minutes
  4. Serve with fresh berries
  5. Enjoy this guilt-free treat!
Breakfast

Green Power Smoothie

Energizing smoothie with spinach, mango, and chia seeds

⏱️ 5 min 1 serving Easy

Green Power Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 cup frozen mango
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup coconut water
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tsp spirulina (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients to blender
  2. Blend on high until smooth
  3. Add more liquid if too thick
  4. Pour into glass
  5. Enjoy immediately for best taste!
